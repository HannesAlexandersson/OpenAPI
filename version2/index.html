Find A Meal

Find a meal is a website where you can search for food and get the recipe for it. You can also get a random meal if you don't know what you want to eat. Perfect for situations when you dont have much time,or just out of imagination. Its all free to use and you dont need to create an account. All cred to the API goes to TheMealDB for providing the API. To use the service, just search for a meal in the search bar, either by the meal name, or bu the main ingredients. Or press the random button to randomly generate a recipe. You can also press the show all categories button to show all the categories and filter by area of origin. Have fun!
